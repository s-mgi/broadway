Explore Life Surrounded By Natural Beauty

Welcome to Broadway Glen, a community where small-town charm meets scenic beauty in the heart of Orangeville. You're surrounded by the natural wonders that make this area so special — from the tranquil waters of Island Lake to the breathtaking trails of Mono Cliffs Provincial Park.

Spend your mornings on the Vicki Barron Lakeside Trail, your afternoons exploring Rotary Park, or unwind with a peaceful walk through Kay Cee Gardens. Whether you're exploring forested trails or relaxing by the lake, Broadway Glen offers a lifestyle where nature is always at your doorstep.

Where Culture Meets Community

In the heart of Orangeville, historic charm meets vibrant local culture. Stroll along Broadway and explore heritage buildings, boutique shops, cozy cafés, and chef-owned restaurants — all with small-town warmth and character. Enjoy live theatre at the Opera House, browse local art studios, or catch seasonal farmers' markets, live music, parades, and community events.

The Best of Both Worlds, Just Steps Away

Broadway Glen brings you closer to everything that makes Orangeville special. Just minutes from your door, explore vibrant downtown streets filled with local shops, dining, and culture. When you're ready to recharge, nature awaits — with scenic trails, green space, and peaceful surroundings all around you.

This thoughtfully planned community blends modern townhome living with the beauty of the outdoors, offering a lifestyle that's active, balanced, and easy to love. Whether it's a morning coffee run or an evening walk in the woods, Broadway Glen puts it all within reach.

Location

Broadway at Dawson Road

Set within a serene, tree-lined enclave near the intersection of Broadway and Dawson Road, Broadway Glen backs onto a scenic natural channel with direct access to the Dufferin County CP Rail Trail and its own private park.

Broadway Glen offers strong connectivity for commuters — with quick access to Highway 10, County Road 109, Highway 9, and routes toward Brampton, Caledon and the GTA. A practical balance between small-town living and regional accessibility, ideal for professionals and hybrid workers alike.
